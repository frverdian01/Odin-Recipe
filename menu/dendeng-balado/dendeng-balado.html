Dendeng Balado

What is Dendeng Balado?

The Padang cuisine version—probably the most popular dendeng dish in Indonesia—is called dendeng balado or dendeng batokok, and is a speciality from Padang, West Sumatra, made from beef which is thinly cut then dried and fried before adding chillies and other ingredients.

    Ingredient

  • 400 g non-fat beef, sliced thinly
  • 1 tablespoon of lime juice marinate
  • 2 tablespoon of lime juice for the sauce
  • 3 shallots
  • 6-8 red chillies, seeded grind roughly
  • 2 fresh kaffir lime leafs
  • salt

    Making Dendeng Balado

  1. Marinate the meat with lime juice . Leave for 15 minutes
  2. Put the meat into oven tray pan and bake it until half dry
  3. When it's done, fry the meat. Set aside
  4. In remaining oil, saute the shallots until fragrant , after that add kaffir lime leafs and chillies. Stir well. And the lime juice , salt and the meat . Continue cooking . Add a little water of it necessary..
